SPEAKER #1

Rauno Freiberg
Veriff / Tallinn
Software Engineer
Measuring the Impact of a Design System
Everyone is building a design system, but what about measuring it? While building Genoma, Veriff's design system, I realized that we should have a way to see it mature over time. I’ll dive deep into my experience of creating a metrics dashboard for tracking the adoption and impact of a design system
SPEAKER #2

Anton Kastritskiy
Yandex.Market / Moscow
Frontend developer
DX & application styling
Let’s take a look of the tools and abstraction the community used to style react applications over the years. Highlight what we have learned from these tools and look into how we solved the styling complexity issue at Yandex Market design system.
